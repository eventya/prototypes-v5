mac-mini-office last checked in 14 minutes ago. Nothing can be built until it's back.
Deploying is blocked on purpose — queueing a build nobody will pick up just leaves you waiting for something that will never happen.

Usual causes, in order of likelihood
  • — The Mac mini is asleep, rebooting, or off. Wake it; the runner starts itself at login.
  • — It lost internet. It only needs outgoing access to eventya.net.
  • — Somebody logged the machine out. The runner lives in the logged-in session — it needs a user logged in to sign iOS builds.

If it's on and connected, RustDesk in and run:

launchctl kickstart -k gui/$(id -u)/net.eventya.deploy-runner
